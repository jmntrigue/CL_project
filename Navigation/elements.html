What You Need

To be able to join the squad, you will have to have two things, the first is the mobile game Marvel Contest of Champions. the other is Line; a mobile messaging app for your phone or tablet.

Alliance Requirements

Our alliance has a few requirements. To be able to remain a member, all you have to do is abide in them.

Fully Participate in
Alliance Events & Quests

We're a team. If we all work together, we can win some really sweet rewards from the alliance events and quests. However, this often means that you will have to put the alliance's needs ahead of your own. You won't be able to do things at the exact moment you want to do them, but if you can exercise a little patience, we will all reap the benefits. By working together and setting aside our personal goals, we will all become stronger players.

Alliance quests are currently our main area of focus. We want to rank as high as possible to get more shards and even try to win a basic 4 champion for everyone in the group. Therefore, we expect everyone to participate as much as possible during the Alliance Quests. If you will not be available, please let an officer know so we can make accommodations. For tips on maximizing your contribution to the quests, please read the guide.

Our alliance has advanced to the point where the alliance events are almost automatic for us. We can win most of the events without putting much focus on them. However, as a member of the alliance, there are two events that we expect everyone to give their full attention:

  • Duel Skirmish – This one-day event rolls around once each week. During this event everyone must defeat the specified Summoner ten times.
  • Completion – During this three-day event, you must score as many points as possible by completing story quests. This is an event where we can win alpha catalysts plus some nice rank rewards if everyone pitches in.
