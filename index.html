How to Apply

Interested in being part of the "Squad"? To join our alliance, you must meet three requirements:

  1. You must have a player rating of at least 40,000
  2. You must be willing and able to join GroupMe for alliance chat
  3. You must be willing to abide by the TWO RULES that we set for our alliance members (see below)
